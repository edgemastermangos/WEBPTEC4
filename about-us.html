Who are the two main partners involved in developing Naawi-Oodena?

The two main partners involved in developing Naawi-Oodena are Treaty One and Canada Lands Company

What does the name "Naawi-Oodena" represent or symbolize?

It means center of the heart and community in Ojibwe and Anishinaabemowin. (source: APTNews website)

Historical Background

Previous Military Base

The name of the base that was previously located on the site of Naawi-Oodena is Kapyong Barracks. (Canada Lands Company)

Federal Government Announcement

The federal government announced the closure of the military base in June 2004. (Canadian Military History)

Land Control Timeline

It took Treaty 1 First Nations approximately 16 years to gain control of the lands legally. (Government of Canada)

Significance of Legal Battle

This legal battle was significant for Indigenous communities across Canada because it opened the door for other First Nations fighting for their rights with the government. (Olthuis Kleer Townshend LLP)
